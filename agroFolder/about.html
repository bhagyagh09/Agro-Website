Vision & Mision

AGSPL has created a separate plant Breeding and Biotechnology research facility in geography Teaneck of NJ State. The research division is recognized by Department of Scientific and Industrial Research, Ministry of Science and Technology, California. The State of the Art Biotechnology Research Center has been affiliated to Fairleigh Dickinson University, NJ. Keeping in mind about the up-coming technologies and gene revolution, GGSPL falls into development of Genetically Modified crops for pest, insect and herbicide resistance. Thus, we are increasing the scope of breeding research, multi-location testing and biotechnology research activities.

Agro Gold Seeds Products are identified for their excellent quality. The seed Production of different crops and their processing packaging of seeds are undertaken as per the norms of Indian minimum seed certification standards through expert quality control team of the company. The main object of the company is to produce and make available, high quality seeds to the farmers at reasonable price.

The company has established Information Technology Facility at all the offices and developed management information system software to support research, production, processing and marketing. The company have own in house website and net working of all operations.

Presently R & D activities being conducted on more than 24 crops including Cotton, Cereals, Vegetables, Oil Seeds, Pulses and Fruit crops. This has enabled us to decorate basket of products portfolio with more than 50 research products.

Efforts of our research scientists are helping us to develop and release genetically modified products with in-built tolerance/resistance against pest, diseases and drought stress conditions. Further, research departments ensure for better produce from our research products.
